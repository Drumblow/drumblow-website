# Drumblow Invoice

Complete billing and financial management solution built for the Canadian market, with subscription support, in-app purchases and compliance.

## Stack

- **Cross-platform**: Flutter (Android, iOS, Web, potential desktop)
- **Backend**: Rust (Axum + SQLx)
- **Payments & Subs**: Stripe + Google Play Billing
- **Features**: Freemium / Trial, magic links, rate limiting, monotonic invoice counters, jobs/clients, printing

## Quality Highlights

- Complete pentest (125 tests) + security audit with 21 vulnerabilities handled
- Play Store listing, privacy policy and data safety declaration ready
- Migrations including trial, freemium, refresh tokens and plan changes
- Layouts designed for desktop and mobile
